Egg Rice

Description

Egg Rice is a simple Korean dish that is a staple for many Koreans

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Steps

  1. Place butter on medium heat pan
  2. Add 2eggs to the pan once the butter melts and scramble
  3. Place the scrambled eggs on rice
  4. Add a tablespoon of soy sauce and mix everything together
  5. Enjoy!
