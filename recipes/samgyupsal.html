Samgupsal Recipe

Description

Korean BBQ dish with the most popular cut of pork

Ingredients

  • Half pound sliced pork belly
  • 2 cups Kimchi
  • 1 cup Garlic

Steps

  1. Heat up pan and add the pork belly
  2. Cook the meat until one side is golden brown and flip it
  3. Once both sides of the pork belly are golden brown, cut the meat into bite size pieces
  4. Add the kimchi and garlic in the pan and cook them in the oil from the pork belly
  5. Enjoy!
