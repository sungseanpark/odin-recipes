Egg Rice

Description

Kimchi Fried Rice is a quick and easy to make rice dish that is also delicious

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 cup kimchi
  • half can spam

Steps

  1. Cut spam and kimchi into bite size pieces
  2. Add a tablespoon of cooking oil into pan and heat the pan up
  3. Add the spam pieces into the pan and cook until all sides are golden brown
  4. Add the kimchi into the pan and cook until its color changes to yellowish orange
  5. Add the rice and stir fry everything together
  6. Add a tablespoon of cooking oil into a different pan and fry an egg to your preference
  7. Move the fried rice into a bowl and place the egg on top
  8. Enjoy!
